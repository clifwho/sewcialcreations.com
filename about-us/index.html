About Us

Hi! I am Barbie Martin the owner of Sewcial Creations. I started my business during the Covid-19 crisis after losing my job like so many others. I have been sewing my whole life for my family and friends and making fun things for my grandchildren. I began making masks for friends and family and soon had orders from businesses and hospitals as word spread. With the help of my family we have expanded to putting logos on masks, T-shirts, tumblers, and more!

Items such as logo masks are carefully custom-made to the customer's desire and we request that the customer approves of the final design before finalizing it. I take pride in my work and will do my best to ensure that the customer is happy.
